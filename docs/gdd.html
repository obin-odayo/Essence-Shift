GDD Abstract

Usually in platformer puzzle games, dying means that you have to restart the level. But in Essence Shift the only way to finish a level is by making an enemy kill you so you can be that enemy. The game takes many inspirations from other innovative puzzle games, esecially Baba is You. However, unlike Baba is You, the game uses enemies as its central puzzle solving tool.

Introduction

Mini Champs is a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) and tower defense hybrid game where a player-controlled character, called the Mini Champs. Defends their crystal from enemies rushing to it from three or more lanes. The player can move their chosen Mini Champs around and kill enemies, deploy, and buff deployed allies.
